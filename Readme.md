# 💫 About Me:
🚀 I build robots that actually work in the real world — not just in simulations. As a mechatronics engineer, I combine mechanical design, electronics, and software to create scalable, high-impact systems, from autonomous mobile robots to VR-enabled robotic arms. I’ve engineered cost-efficient mechanisms like a cycloidal drive with 90% reduction in cost, integrated ROS2 with real-time digital twins, and developed automation systems using PLCs and embedded hardware. With a strong foundation across CAD, control systems, and AI-driven robotics, I focus on moving fast from concept to working product — building, breaking, and refining until it performs. Currently exploring advanced robotics research and intelligent systems, with the goal of creating technology that doesn’t just exist, but delivers.
